Of Counsel

Warren J. Widmayer

Warren handles estate planning and employee benefits law matters. Following the death of Larry Ferguson, partner and co-founder of the firm, Warren assumed leadership of the firm's estate planning practice, particularly for estates with potential estate tax liability.

Warren brings decades of experience to his practice, combining deep expertise in estate planning with specialized knowledge of employee benefits law. His dual focus allows him to provide comprehensive counsel to clients navigating complex financial and succession planning matters.

Estate Planning Leadership

Warren's leadership of the firm's estate planning practice reflects his extensive experience and reputation in this area. He is particularly skilled in handling high-value estates with potential estate tax implications, working with clients to develop strategies that minimize tax burden while achieving their estate planning objectives.

Employee Benefits Expertise

Warren's practice in employee benefits law, including ERISA matters, provides valuable counsel to businesses establishing and maintaining retirement and benefit plans. His expertise helps employers navigate the complex regulatory landscape governing employee benefits.
