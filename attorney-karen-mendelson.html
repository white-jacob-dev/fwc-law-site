Karen T. Mendelson

A graduate of the University of Michigan Law School, Karen has practiced law in Ann Arbor, Michigan for over 30 years. Her practice focuses primarily on real estate and small business transactions.

Karen has worked extensively with people buying, selling, and refinancing residential and commercial real estate. Her decades of experience in the Ann Arbor market gives her invaluable insight into local real estate practices and the specific challenges that buyers and sellers face in this community.

Real Estate Practice

Karen's real estate practice encompasses all aspects of property transactions, from initial contract negotiation through closing. She represents buyers, sellers, and lenders in residential and commercial transactions, ensuring that her clients' interests are protected throughout the process.

1031 Exchange Expertise

Karen also owns and operates Property Exchange Company, serving as a qualified intermediary in 1031 like-kind exchanges. This specialized expertise allows her to help real estate investors defer capital gains taxes through properly structured exchange transactions.

Small Business Counsel

Beyond real estate, Karen provides counsel to small businesses on a variety of transactional matters. Her practical approach helps business owners navigate legal requirements while focusing on their core business objectives.
