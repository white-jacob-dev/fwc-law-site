Mark Hopper

Mark A. Hopper has over 30 years of experience in civil trial practice in State and Federal courts. He also works in arbitration, mediation and other forms of alternative dispute resolution and concentrates his work in the areas of complex contract, commercial, and real estate matters.

Mark brings decades of courtroom experience to his practice, providing clients with the skilled advocacy they need when disputes arise. His extensive trial experience in both state and federal courts gives him a comprehensive understanding of litigation strategy and procedure.

Civil Litigation

Mark's civil litigation practice focuses on complex commercial disputes, including contract matters and real estate litigation. He has successfully represented clients in high-stakes cases, developing strategies that protect their interests and achieve favorable outcomes.

Alternative Dispute Resolution

Recognizing that litigation is not always the most efficient path to resolution, Mark also has extensive experience in arbitration, mediation, and other forms of alternative dispute resolution. These skills allow him to help clients resolve disputes more quickly and cost-effectively when appropriate.

Complex Commercial Matters

Mark's concentration in complex contract and commercial matters means he understands the nuances of business disputes. Whether negotiating a resolution or preparing for trial, he brings strategic thinking and practical experience to every case.
