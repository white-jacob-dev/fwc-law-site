Of Counsel

Mark Hopper

Mark A. Hopper has over 30 years of experience in arbitration, mediation, and other forms of alternative dispute resolution. He concentrates his practice in the areas of complex contract, commercial, and real estate matters.

Mark brings decades of experience to his practice, providing clients with skilled advocacy in resolving disputes efficiently and effectively. His comprehensive understanding of dispute resolution strategies helps clients achieve favorable outcomes without the cost and time of traditional court proceedings.

Alternative Dispute Resolution

Mark has extensive experience in arbitration, mediation, and other forms of alternative dispute resolution. These skills allow him to help clients resolve disputes more quickly and cost-effectively, achieving results that satisfy all parties involved.

Complex Commercial Matters

Mark's concentration in complex contract and commercial matters means he understands the nuances of business disputes. Whether negotiating a resolution or guiding clients through arbitration, he brings strategic thinking and practical experience to every case.

Real Estate Disputes

With extensive experience in real estate matters, Mark helps clients resolve property-related disputes through mediation and arbitration, finding solutions that protect their interests while preserving important business relationships.
