Estate Planning & Business Succession

Comprehensive planning to protect your assets and ensure your wishes are honored.

Estate planning is one of the most important steps you can take to protect yourself, your loved ones, and your assets. At Ferguson Widmayer & Clark PC, we work closely with each client to develop customized estate plans that reflect their unique circumstances, goals, and values.

Our Estate Planning Services

We provide comprehensive estate planning services designed to protect your legacy and provide for your loved ones. Our experienced attorneys will guide you through every step of the process, ensuring your plan addresses all aspects of your personal and financial situation.

  • Wills: Foundational documents that direct how your assets will be distributed and who will care for minor children.
  • Revocable Living Trusts: Flexible arrangements that can help avoid probate and provide for management of assets during incapacity.
  • Irrevocable Trusts: Specialized trusts for asset protection, tax planning, and special needs planning.
  • Powers of Attorney: Documents that authorize trusted individuals to make financial and legal decisions on your behalf.
  • Healthcare Directives: Instructions for medical care and end-of-life decisions, including patient advocate designations.
  • Beneficiary Designations: Review and coordination of retirement accounts, life insurance, and other assets.

Business Succession Planning

For business owners, succession planning is essential to ensuring the continuity and success of your enterprise. We help you develop comprehensive succession strategies that address ownership transfer, management transition, and tax implications.

  • Buy-Sell Agreements: Contracts that govern the transfer of business interests upon death, disability, or departure.
  • Family Business Succession: Structured plans for transferring ownership and management to the next generation.
  • Key Employee Retention: Strategies to retain and reward key employees during ownership transitions.
  • Business Valuation Coordination: Working with valuation experts to establish fair market value for planning purposes.

Why Estate Planning Matters

A well-crafted estate plan does more than distribute assets—it provides peace of mind, minimizes family conflict, reduces tax burdens, and ensures your wishes are carried out exactly as intended. Without proper planning, state law will determine how your assets are distributed, often with results that don't align with your intentions.

Our attorneys take the time to understand your complete picture—your family dynamics, financial situation, business interests, and personal goals—to create a plan that truly serves your needs.
