Michael D. Shelton

Mike is a managing shareholder who joined Ferguson Widmayer & Clark PC in 2015. Mike focuses his practice on estate planning, trust and estate administration, wealth and tax planning for high-net-worth clients, and elder law.

Mike brings a comprehensive approach to estate planning, working closely with clients to understand their unique family dynamics, financial situations, and long-term goals. His expertise in wealth and tax planning makes him particularly valuable to high-net-worth individuals and families seeking to preserve and transfer wealth efficiently.

In his role as managing shareholder, Mike helps guide the strategic direction of the firm while maintaining an active client practice. He is committed to providing personalized service and ensuring that each client receives the attention and expertise their legal matters deserve.

Elder Law Practice

Mike has developed particular expertise in elder law, helping families navigate the complex legal and financial challenges that arise as loved ones age. This includes planning for long-term care, Medicaid planning, guardianship and conservatorship matters, and protecting assets while ensuring quality care for elderly family members.
