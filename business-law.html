Employment & Business Law

Strategic counsel for businesses of all sizes, from formation to ongoing operations.

Whether you're launching a new venture or managing an established enterprise, sound legal counsel is essential to your success. At Ferguson Widmayer & Clark PC, we provide strategic business law services that help you navigate challenges, seize opportunities, and protect your interests.

Business Formation & Structure

Choosing the right business structure is one of the most important decisions you'll make as an entrepreneur. We help you understand the implications of each option and select the structure that best serves your goals.

  • Entity Selection: Analysis of LLCs, corporations, partnerships, and other structures based on liability protection, tax implications, and operational needs.
  • Formation Documents: Preparation of articles of organization, operating agreements, bylaws, and shareholder agreements.
  • Business Licensing: Guidance on required licenses, permits, and regulatory compliance.
  • Corporate Governance: Establishing proper procedures for meetings, record-keeping, and decision-making.

Contract Drafting & Review

Well-drafted contracts protect your business relationships and minimize disputes. Our attorneys prepare and review a wide range of commercial agreements.

  • Commercial Contracts: Vendor agreements, service contracts, licensing arrangements, and distribution agreements.
  • Partnership & Operating Agreements: Documents governing relationships between business owners.
  • Purchase & Sale Agreements: Contracts for buying or selling business assets or entire enterprises.
  • Non-Compete & Confidentiality Agreements: Protection of trade secrets and competitive interests.

Employment Law

Managing employment relationships requires careful attention to legal requirements and best practices. We help employers establish compliant policies and navigate workplace issues.

  • Employment Agreements: Executive contracts, offer letters, and independent contractor agreements.
  • Employee Handbooks: Comprehensive policy manuals that establish expectations and protect employers.
  • Workplace Policies: Anti-discrimination, harassment prevention, leave policies, and workplace safety.
  • Termination Matters: Guidance on proper procedures, severance agreements, and risk mitigation.

Business Transactions & Disputes

When business deals require negotiation or disputes arise, you need experienced counsel who understands both the legal landscape and business realities.

  • Mergers & Acquisitions: Due diligence, negotiation, and documentation for buying or selling businesses.
  • Commercial Real Estate: Lease negotiations, purchase agreements, and zoning matters.
  • Business Disputes: Resolution of contract disputes, partnership disagreements, and other commercial conflicts.
