Eric Sloat

Eric has been an attorney with Ferguson Widmayer & Clark PC since 2017. He works in a variety of practice areas including estate planning, probate, trust administration, business law, and real estate, all of which relate to helping local families and their businesses.

Eric's practice primarily focuses on business law, real estate law, and assisting families with their legal needs. He brings a client-centered approach to his work, taking the time to understand each client's unique situation and goals.

Business & Real Estate Focus

Eric helps business owners navigate the legal complexities of running and growing their companies. From business formation and contracts to real estate transactions and commercial matters, he provides practical, strategic counsel that helps clients make informed decisions.

Commitment to Community

As an attorney dedicated to serving local families and businesses, Eric understands the importance of accessible, personalized legal services. He is committed to building lasting relationships with his clients and providing the attentive representation that important legal matters deserve.
