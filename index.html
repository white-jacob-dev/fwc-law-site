Our Firm

A Legacy of Excellence in Legal Practice

Ferguson Widmayer & Clark PC has served the Ann Arbor community and surrounding areas with distinction, building lasting relationships founded on trust, integrity, and exceptional legal counsel.

Our attorneys combine deep legal expertise with a genuine commitment to understanding each client's unique circumstances. We believe that effective representation begins with listening—taking the time to fully comprehend your goals, concerns, and the nuances of your situation.

Whether you are planning for your family's future, navigating complex business matters, or settling an estate, we provide the careful attention and sound judgment that important legal matters demand.
