Nicole Appleberry

Nicole is currently Clinical Assistant Professor of Law at the University of Michigan Law School, where she runs the Low-Income Taxpayer Clinic and other clinical programs. She also handles select tax matters for the firm as her schedule allows.

Nicole brings a unique combination of academic expertise and practical experience to her role at Ferguson Widmayer & Clark PC. Her work at the University of Michigan Law School provides her with deep insight into tax law issues, which she applies in her practice with the firm.

Academic Leadership

As Clinical Assistant Professor of Law, Nicole directs the Low-Income Taxpayer Clinic at the University of Michigan Law School. This clinic provides free legal assistance to low-income taxpayers who have disputes with the IRS, while also training the next generation of tax attorneys. Her leadership of clinical programs demonstrates her commitment to both legal education and public service.

Tax Law Practice

Nicole's expertise in tax law allows her to assist clients with complex tax matters. Her academic background combined with practical experience provides clients with sophisticated counsel on tax-related issues.
