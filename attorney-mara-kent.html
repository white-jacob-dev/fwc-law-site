Mara Kent

Mara Kent (she/her/hers) is a life-long resident of Ann Arbor, and her practice areas include estate planning, probate and trust administration, elder law and long-term care planning, special needs planning, and estate planning for surrogacy and assisted reproduction.

As a lifelong member of the Ann Arbor community, Mara brings both professional expertise and personal investment to her work with local families. She understands the unique needs of this community and is deeply committed to helping her clients navigate complex legal matters with care and compassion.

Elder Law & Long-Term Care Planning

Mara has developed expertise in elder law, helping families plan for the challenges that come with aging. Her practice includes Medicaid planning, long-term care planning, and helping families protect assets while ensuring their loved ones receive quality care.

Special Needs Planning

Mara is committed to helping families with members who have special needs. She works with families to create comprehensive plans that protect eligibility for government benefits while ensuring the individual's quality of life and long-term security.

Surrogacy & Assisted Reproduction

Recognizing the evolving ways families are formed, Mara provides estate planning services specifically tailored to families created through surrogacy and assisted reproduction. She helps ensure that these families have proper legal protections in place from the start.
