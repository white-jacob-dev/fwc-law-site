Miriam Saffo

Miriam joined Ferguson Widmayer & Clark PC in 2023. Before joining FWC she spent the first five years of her career practicing in the area of family law.

She was drawn to estate planning as a way to help people in a meaningful way, a goal she has held since the beginning of her legal career. Miriam is dedicated to helping individuals and families plan for life's important events and ensure their wishes are properly documented and protected.

Transition to Estate Planning

Miriam's background in family law provides her with valuable insight into family dynamics and the importance of clear, thoughtful planning. She understands how legal decisions impact families and brings this perspective to her estate planning practice.

Professional Leadership

Miriam is active in the State Bar of Michigan Young Lawyers Section Council, demonstrating her commitment to the legal profession and her desire to contribute to its development. This involvement keeps her connected to emerging trends in legal practice and provides opportunities to mentor newer attorneys.
