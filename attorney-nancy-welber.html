Nancy H. Welber

Nancy Welber is an attorney and a CPA. She has been practicing law since 1982. Nancy concentrates in the areas of estate and retirement planning, both as the primary and consulting attorney, with considerable experience in the area of planning with TIAA-CREF and other 403(b) annuities.

Nancy's dual credentials as both an attorney and a Certified Public Accountant give her unique insight into the intersection of legal and financial planning. Her decades of experience have made her a trusted advisor for clients navigating complex estate and retirement planning decisions.

Estate & Retirement Planning

Nancy's practice focuses on helping clients plan for the transfer of their wealth and the security of their retirement years. She works with clients to develop comprehensive plans that address their unique circumstances, goals, and family dynamics.

TIAA-CREF & 403(b) Expertise

Nancy has developed considerable expertise in planning with TIAA-CREF and other 403(b) annuities, making her particularly valuable to educators, researchers, and others in the nonprofit sector who hold these retirement accounts. She understands the unique rules and opportunities associated with these plans and helps clients maximize their benefits.

Consulting Services

In addition to serving as primary counsel, Nancy also provides consulting services to other attorneys on estate and retirement planning matters. Her deep expertise in these areas makes her a valuable resource for colleagues handling complex planning issues.
