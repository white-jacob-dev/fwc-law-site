Our Firm

Our Story

A legacy of excellence in legal practice, built on trust and dedication to our clients.

For decades, Ferguson Widmayer & Clark PC has been a cornerstone of the Ann Arbor legal community, providing thoughtful, personalized legal services to individuals, families, and businesses throughout Michigan.

Our firm was founded on a simple principle: that exceptional legal counsel begins with genuinely understanding each client's unique circumstances, goals, and concerns. This philosophy has guided us through generations of practice and remains at the heart of everything we do.

"We believe that effective representation begins with listening—taking the time to fully comprehend your goals, concerns, and the nuances of your situation."

Our Approach

At Ferguson Widmayer & Clark PC, we combine deep legal expertise with a genuine commitment to building lasting relationships with our clients. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all solutions. Instead, we take the time to understand the complete picture—your family dynamics, financial situation, business interests, and personal goals—to develop strategies that truly serve your needs.

Our attorneys bring decades of combined experience across estate planning, business law, and probate matters. This breadth of knowledge allows us to provide comprehensive counsel that addresses all aspects of our clients' legal needs, often anticipating issues before they arise.

Our Commitment

We are committed to providing our clients with:

  • Discretion: Your matters are handled with the utmost confidentiality and care.
  • Experience: Decades of practice across estate planning, business, and probate law.
  • Dedication: Personalized attention to every client, every matter.
  • Integrity: Honest, straightforward advice you can trust.

Our Home

Our offices are located at 538 North Division Street in Ann Arbor, in a historic building that reflects our firm's commitment to tradition, quality, and enduring service. We welcome you to visit and discover why generations of Michigan families and businesses have trusted Ferguson Widmayer & Clark PC with their most important legal matters.

We honor the memory of our founding partners, Larry Ferguson and Walter B. Ranney, whose vision and dedication established the foundation upon which our firm continues to build.
